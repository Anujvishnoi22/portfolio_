🛒 RFID Shopping Trolley

An RFID shopping trolley is a smart cart equipped with Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology to streamline the shopping process. Each product has an RFID tag, and the trolley is fitted with an RFID reader that automatically detects the items as they are placed in or removed from the cart. The trolley displays the total bill in real time, reducing the need for manual scanning at checkout, making shopping faster and more efficient
